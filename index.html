About SSN ACM

The SSN ACM Student Chapter, is an official student body functioning under the patronage of the Computer Science and Engineering Department. Since its inception in 2012, the club has been steadily working to inculcate an unalloyed interest for Computer Science in students and consequently, stimulating the advancement of computer science as a whole.The student chapter helps nurture the student community with pragmatic know-how and offers professional networking opportunities by organizing presentations and discussions with distinguished subject experts, site visits to industrial facilities and research labs, educational workshops, contests to honor excellence and a wide array of other events. Our carefully curated digital library featuring over 600,000 research articles and TechTalks, our online talk series featuring visionary researchers from in dustry and academia, offer an abundance of learning re ources and professional wisdom to the budding fellows of the computing field.
